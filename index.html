Five Reasons to Lucid Dream

...and why you should try it

Go on Adventures

You can create wild dreamscapes of your favorite stories or make up your own as you go. This allows you to become creative and push the boundaries of logic.

Troubleshoot Problems

In this state of mind, you are given an opportunity to think outside the box, come up with new solutions, and gain perspectives on how to tackle those pesky issues that were bothering you.

Overcome Fears

In this realm, your physical body is safe. Let your mind create the fears that affect you and slowly learn to overcome them. Once you realize you are in a safe place, these fears become easier to conquer.

Explore your inner self

Find the truth of who you are. Advanced dreamers can manifest their subconscious and have conversations with it. Navigate your inner mind and learn why you do what you do, asking yourself who you want to be.

Meet version of your loved ones

Death can be a tragedy for many, especially when we didn't get to say our goodbyes. Meet a version of your father, friend, lover, and have a conversation with them, expressing how much you miss and love them.
